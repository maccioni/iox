  tar xzf ioxclient_1.5.1.1_linux_amd64.tar.gz

</li>

<li><p>Install qemu-utils to convert VM file system from vmdk to qcow2 format</p>

<pre><code class="bash language-bash"># Ubuntu

</code></pre></li>

<pre><code class="bash language-bash"># Mac
